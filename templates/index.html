Welcome to our Weather API

To ge the temperature of the station in the exact date place the following URL

The URL: http://127.0.0.1:5000/api/v1/< station_code >/< YY-MM-DD >
(eg: http://127.0.0.1:5000/api/v1/10/1990-05-14

To ge the temperature of the station for all years

The URL: http://127.0.0.1:5000/api/v1/< station_code >
(eg: http://127.0.0.1:5000/api/v1/10

To ge the temperature of the station for all a particular year

The URL: http://127.0.0.1:5000/api/v1/yearly/< YY-MM-DD >
(eg: http://127.0.0.1:5000/api/v1/yearly/2000

Note: Please use available station id and date in (YY-MM-DD) format (eg:1983-08-05)
